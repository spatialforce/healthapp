Welcome to Spatial Force

About Us

Spatial Force is a pioneering organization dedicated to advancing Geographic Information Systems (GIS) development. Founded by Kudzanai Chakavarika, we specialize in creating interactive web maps and tackling a wide range of GIS challenges, including spatial analysis and technical problem-solving. With expertise in both front-end and back-end development, as well as hands-on technical skills, Spatial Force is committed to delivering comprehensive GIS services that empower clients to visualize and analyze spatial data effectively. Our mission is to leverage the power of GIS technology to drive informed decision-making and foster sustainable growth in communities. At Spatial Force, we believe in the transformative potential of spatial intelligence and strive to make it accessible to all.

What we Do

As skilled developers based in Bulawayo, Zimbabwe, at Spatial Force, we excel in utilizing a diverse range of front-end and back-end technologies to create innovative solutions. Proficient in Django, GeoDjango, and PostGIS, we leverage Python and Django REST Framework to build robust applications that interact seamlessly with data. Our front-end expertise includes JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Leaflet, Turf.js, and Bootstrap, enabling us to create engaging and interactive web applications and maps. We also do spatial analysis and creation of various vainteractive maps, as well utilizing remote sensing indices such as EVI, NDVI, SAVI, and NDWI in environmental management. Additionally, we conduct land cover change mapping and change detection analysis using tools like Google Earth Engine (GEE), QGIS, ArcMap, and ENVI. Our work also extend to data cleaning and preparation, ensuring high-quality data for analysis. With a passion for GIS and a commitment to problem-solving, We tackle a wide array of challenges in the field, making significant contributions to our projects.

Mission

At Spatial Force, our mission is to harness the power of Geographic Information Systems (GIS) to provide innovative solutions that enhance spatial analysis and decision-making. We strive to empower individuals and organizations with the tools they need to effectively visualize, interpret, and utilize spatial data, ultimately contributing to sustainable development and improved community outcomes.
