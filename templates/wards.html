Wards of Bulawayo

Bulawayo, Zimbabwe's second-largest city, is divided into 29 administrative wards. Each ward serves as a unique community, encompassing various neighborhoods, facilities, and local services. Understanding these wards is essential for civic engagement and community development.

Ward 1

Features: CBD, Parkview, Paddonhurst, Sunnyside, Northend.

Ward 2

Features: Airport, Barbourfields, Glenville, Harrisvale.

Ward 3

Features: Queenspark East, Parklands, Mahatshula North and South.

Ward 4

Features: Buena Vista, Kabanga, Ilanda, Lochview.

Ward 5

Features: Greenhill, Morningside, Southwold.

Ward 6

Features: Cowdray Park Phase 2 and Western Portion of Phase 4.

Ward 7

Ward 8

Ward 9
